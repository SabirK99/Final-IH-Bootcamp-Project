  • About the Author

"Psycho-Cybernetics" delves into the idea of the human mind as a self-guiding target system, moving individuals toward the realization of their goals. Maltz presents concepts of self-image, mental rehearsal, and self-affirmation, emphasizing the power of positive thinking and visualization in achieving personal goals.

Dr. Maxwell Maltz was a cosmetic surgeon who transitioned into psychology and motivational writing after observing the impact of self-image on his patients' lives. His pioneering work in self-image and self-improvement became a cornerstone in the field of personal development.
