In "Can't Hurt Me", David Goggins narrates his incredible life journey, from facing poverty and prejudice to becoming a Navy SEAL, world record holder, and ultra-endurance athlete. The book delves deep into his philosophy of pushing past pain, fear, and limits to achieve personal greatness.

David Goggins is an American ultra-endurance athlete, motivational speaker, and former Navy SEAL. Known for his astounding physical feats and mental toughness, Goggins has completed numerous endurance events and set world records. Beyond his athletic accomplishments, he's recognized for his candid exploration of personal transformation and resilience.
